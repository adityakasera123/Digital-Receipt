# Digital Receipt Vault

## Personal Purchase Management System

# Vision

Digital Receipt Vault ek modern web-based **Personal Purchase Management System** hai jo users ko unki purchases, receipts, invoices, warranty documents aur purchase history ko ek centralized aur secure platform par manage karne ki suvidha deta hai.

Aaj ke digital world me log Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, local stores, restaurants, electronics shops aur service centers se regularly shopping karte hain. Har purchase ke saath receipt, invoice ya warranty document milta hai, jo email, SMS, WhatsApp, PDF ya paper form me alag-alag jagah par scattered rehta hai.

Jab warranty claim, product replacement, return request, refund request ya expense analysis ki zarurat padti hai, tab users ko receipts dhoondhne me kaafi dikkat hoti hai.

Digital Receipt Vault ka objective hai ki purchase-related har important document ko ek intelligent platform par organize kiya ja sake jahan user sirf receipts store hi na kare, balki apni purchasing history ko samjhe, return period manage kare, warranty track kare aur future me AI-powered insights bhi receive kare.

Ye sirf ek Receipt Storage Application nahi hoga, balki ek complete **Purchase Lifecycle Management Platform** banne ka vision rakhta hai.

---

# Problem Statement

Current market me users ko kai practical problems face karni padti hain:

* Purchase receipts alag-alag platforms par scattered hoti hain.
* Paper receipts time ke saath kharab ya lost ho jaati hain.
* Warranty claim ke time receipt nahi milti.
* Return window miss ho jaati hai.
* Product defective hone ke baad bhi users return period bhool jaate hain.
* Original packaging return period khatam hone se pehle hi discard kar di jaati hai.
* Expense tracking difficult hota hai.
* Purchase history properly maintain nahi hoti.
* Insurance ya service ke time invoice dhoondhna padta hai.
* Important purchase documents secure location par available nahi hote.
* Users ke paas purchase se lekar warranty aur service tak ka complete record maintain karne ka koi centralized platform nahi hota.

---

# Proposed Solution

Digital Receipt Vault ek centralized platform provide karega jahan users:

* Receipts upload kar sakte hain.
* Purchase history maintain kar sakte hain.
* Return window track kar sakte hain.
* Warranty expiration monitor kar sakte hain.
* Purchase documents securely store kar sakte hain.
* Smart search se receipt instantly dhoondh sakte hain.
* Expense analytics dekh sakte hain.
* AI-powered purchase insights receive kar sakte hain.

Ye sirf ek receipt storage application nahi, balki complete Personal Purchase Management System hoga jo purchase ki complete lifecycle ko manage karega.
